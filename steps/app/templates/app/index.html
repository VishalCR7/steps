About us

Based in Bangalore, termed the Silicon Valley of India, S.T.E.P.S helps high-potential start-ups scale and get discovered by a network of incubators, entrepreneurs and a network of other influencers. We’ve guided hundreds of startups in building products and viable business models using our platforms, processes and people and are currently in the third year of our operation connecting startups with incubators that are happy to take them on.

More...

What we provide ?

We aim to provide a platform for fledgling startups to connect with experienced incubators in order to be able to better market their products by utilizing the considerable infrastructure that the incubators have to offer. It also helps the incubators better decide from amongst the vast number of choices that they are presented with so that they can forge a partnership that could be mutually beneficial to both the involved parties.

Our vision

We are a bunch of sophomores from IIT Roorkee who believe that there are many like us who have brilliant ideas to make this world a better place to live in. We believe that incuatinbg a startup is very similar to raising a baby, requiring a lot of care and assistance to ease the “growing” pains, and hence we provide the best incubators for your fledgling organization

Why should you choose us ?

Well it is simple, this is the only platform till date which directly helps you interact with the incubators and meet the requirements a startup has. If this does not convince you, ask any of our successful 1200 startups which have found the perfect incubators and are well on their way to becoming successful companies in their own right.
