About us

We set out as a two man show, from the premier BITS-Pilani, an idea with us and no more than that. Having been laughed off as mere dreamers, our determination to launch this platform strengthened by the influx of collaborators from IIT Roorkee. Our goal? Let no idea die due to a lack of support and resources. And this platform is a realisation of that dream.

More...

What we provide ?

coSax provides an online platform for the young entrepreneurial talents to share their idea,team up with people wo are excited about what you share and start your own venture.We believe that the young entrepreneurs have million dollar idea and those shouldn't die because of lack of resources.

Our vision

We are a bunch of young entrepreneurs from BITS Pilani and IIT Roorkee who believe that there are many like us who have brilliant ideas to make this world a better place to live in. We believe that an idea is only good if it comes out and gets executed well. Therefore we are here to provide opportunity to every entrepreneur to come up with an idea and find like minded people to make it big.

Why should you choose us ?

Well it is simple, we will provide you with all the resources be it expert advice or investors to help you all the way through your startup journey. Moreover you are teaming up with people who share the same interests as yours so there is no looking back. Come here,get inspired from the crowd and start your own venture.
